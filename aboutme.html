About Me

I am a general geek with interest in all things Infrastructure, DevOps and Cybersecurity with expertise in threat hunting, automation, and project management. I specialize in securely deploying, monitoring, and maintaining large-scale hybrid Infrastructures, including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, VMware vSphere, and Kubernetes.

My experience also includes network-based firewalls, site-to-site VPN deployment, application containerization, and cloud migration. These days I focus mostly on cloud and on-premise infrastructure automation and custom Wordpress theme development, deployment, and security.
